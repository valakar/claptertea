CLAPTER TEA BAG

CLAPTE Pyramid Tea Bags are made of a fine see-through bio degradable mesh material. The pyramid shape is regarded as the optimal teabag design allowing the maximum room for the tea to expand and is perfectly suited for quality loose-leaf teas. The structured shape will prevent tea leaves are not compressed inside the bag and, when the bag is submerged in hot water, the leaves can swell, float freely and infuse maximum flavor into the water. There is less breakage of the tea leaves in these bags, reducing or eliminating the presence of tea “dust” in the bottom of the cup.

Brewing instructions:
Put tea bag into a cup and add freshly boiled water. Steeping time 3-5 minutes
